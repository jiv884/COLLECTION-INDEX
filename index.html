MAP OF LONELINESS

This is the MAP OF LONELINESS.

This is a map of my journey from 2017 to 2019.

I selected 45 photos from the time period that express 3 different status of my "loneliness":

WHEN I'M NOT ALONE - Pictures I took when I was by myself; pure solitude.

WHEN I'M NOT ALONE - Pictures I took with people; companionship.

WHEN I FEEL I'M NOT ALONE - Pictures of films, books or artworks that kept me company when I was alone.

I depicted this map with these moments that recorded my loneliness.
